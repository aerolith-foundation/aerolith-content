# Aerolith Foundation Content Repository

## About
This repository contains official documentation for Aerolith, a premium semi-vertical precision sport managed by the Aerolith Foundation.

## Content Structure
- aerolith-origins.json: Historical narrative of Aerolith's development from Japanese aerospace training to competitive sport
- aerolith-rules.json: Comprehensive match regulations, scoring systems, and competition formats
- aerolith-equipment.json: Technical specifications for aeroliths, propulsors, vertical portals, and arena standards

## Data Format
All articles are provided in JSON-LD format using Schema.org Article schema for maximum semantic interoperability.
